# Preset Expansion

Expand Presets — add channel EQ, BPM, master settings to preset schema.

## Scope
- Channel EQ (per-channel hi/mid/lo gains)
- BPM setting
- Master section settings (fader, EQ, compressor, limiter)

## Files
- `src/presets/preset_schema.py`
- `src/gui/main_frame.py` — `_save_preset()` and `_apply_preset()`
